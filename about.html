ABOUT ME

Born and raised in Estcourt, South Africa, I am an aspiring Cybersecurity Analyst with a diploma in Software Development from Rosebank College. While my professional experience includes sales and graphic design, my true passion lies in the world of cybersecurity. Currently, I am advancing my skills and knowledge through an intensive program at CapaCiTi, where I am working diligently to build a successful career in this dynamic and ever-evolving field. The journey to discovering my passion has been both enriching and transformative, filled with valuable experiences that have shaped the person I am today.

It all began during my high school years, where I developed an interest in technology and problem-solving. After completing matric, I took on a role as a general worker at Gambushe Property, which instilled in me a strong work ethic and adaptability. Driven to pursue my interest in technology, I enrolled at Rosebank College to study Software Development. This was a pivotal moment in my life, as it allowed me to gain technical knowledge while also balancing part-time work as a graphic designer for Logoman Design. These experiences not only honed my creative skills but also taught me the importance of discipline and time management.

After graduating, I moved to Johannesburg in search of better opportunities in my field of study. It was here that I began my professional career as a sales consultant at African Bank. Though sales was not directly aligned with my aspirations, it was a valuable experience that strengthened my communication, customer service, and interpersonal skills. This role also taught me the importance of building relationships, an essential quality in any profession. However, my desire to work in the tech industry remained unwavering, motivating me to seek opportunities that aligned more closely with my interests.

My breakthrough came when I joined CapaCiTi, a program designed to equip individuals with advanced technical skills. Through this training, I was introduced to the fascinating world of cybersecurity, and I quickly realized this was the path I was meant to follow. The program not only deepened my understanding of cybersecurity principles but also allowed me to develop practical skills in areas such as threat detection, risk management, and ethical hacking. It was during this time that I truly fell in love with cybersecurity, captivated by its complexity and the opportunity to make a meaningful impact in protecting digital systems.

Looking back, my journey has been one of growth and self-discovery. Each step, from my early days as a general worker to my current training in cybersecurity, has played a crucial role in shaping my career path. The diverse experiences I have gained along the way have given me a unique perspective and a versatile skill set. Today, I am more determined than ever to excel in the cybersecurity field, driven by a passion for technology and a commitment to safeguarding the digital world. My story is a testament to the power of perseverance, adaptability, and following one's true calling.
